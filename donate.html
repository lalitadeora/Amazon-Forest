DONATION OPTIONS:

SINGLE GIFTS

Help us protect the Amazon rainforest with a single gift.

MONTHLY GIFTS

Help us protect the Amazon rainforest every day of the year – become a monthly donor!

GIFTS OF SECURITIES

By donating stock directly to the Amazon Rainforest Conservancy without cashing it in, you eliminate your capital gains tax bill! You can use prescribed bonds, units of mutual funds, exchange traded funds (ETFs), shares, warrants, bills and futures that are listed on the stock exchange. You’ll also receive a tax receipt for the fair market share value of the asset, which will help offset your income taxes.

EMPLOYER MATCHING GIFTS

Many employers sponsor matching gift programs and will match charitable contributions by their employees. Find out if your employer offers this program and you can double the value of your donation!

CORPORATE SPONSORS

Show your organization’s commitment to conservation and the natural world by becoming one of our corporate sponsors. Your sponsorship will help us purchase and protect more land in the Amazon rainforest.

RAINFOREST AMBASSADORS

Hold a fundraiser to generate money for ARC. Our supporters have held some great fundraisers, including: run-a-thons, bake sales, busking, tattooing and body painting events, carolling for the Amazon, and even a rave for the rainforest! Many supporters ask for donations to ARC in lieu of birthday gifts.

LEGACY GIFTS

By sharing even a small portion of your estate with ARC, you can make a significant and long-term impact on protecting the Amazon rainforest.

Leaving a legacy gift can be as simple as adding a clause to your will. Our partners at Will Power make it easy and can walk you through the steps. Visit their website to access free tools and resources for you to explore. There’s even a legacy calculator which helps you calculate your potential forever impact.
